# AI Engineer | LLM Agent Development | Computer Vision

I'm an AI Engineer at Hexaware Technologies, developing intelligent LLM-based agents for SAP Financial Accounting automation. With expertise spanning computer vision, medical image analysis, and enterprise AI systems, I bridge the gap between cutting-edge research and production-ready solutions.

## Current Focus
- **Enterprise AI Automation**: Building LLM agents for SAP FI module automation using LangChain and FastAPI
- **Computer Vision**: Developing segmentation models for medical imaging analysis
- **Research to Production**: Translating academic research into scalable enterprise solutions

## Technical Expertise

My technical stack spans the full AI/ML development lifecycle:
- **Deep Learning**: PyTorch, PyTorch Lightning, TensorFlow, MONAI
- **Computer Vision**: Medical imaging (DICOM), satellite imagery processing, segmentation models
- **NLP/LLM**: LangChain, Transformers, CLIP, RAG systems
- **Enterprise Integration**: FastAPI, SAP OData APIs, microservices architecture
- **Cloud & DevOps**: Docker, Kubernetes, HPC clusters, distributed computing
